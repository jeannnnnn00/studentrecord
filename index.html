Welcome to Northwest Samar State University

About Us

Northwest Samar State University (NwSSU) began in 1912 as the Samar Trade School, offering vocational training to students. In 1959, it was converted into the Samar School of Arts and Trades (SSAT), expanding its programs to include courses like automotive technology, dressmaking, and carpentry, which helped strengthen the region’s workforce and economy.

Classes and Programs

The university offers programs under seven colleges: the College of Computing, College of Education, College of Nursing, College of Engineering and Architecture, College of Criminal and Justices, College of Agriculture and Technology and College of Management, covering a wide range of undergraduate and graduate degrees.

Contact Us

You may reach Northwest Samar State University at Calbayog City, Western Samar, Philippines 6710. For inquiries, call (055) 000-0000 or send an email to info@nssu.edu.ph. Office hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Class Schedule

The class schedule for the current semester is posted on the bulletin boards of each college. Students may also check their schedules through the Registrar's Office or their respective department heads.

University Library

The NWSSU library is open Monday to Friday from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM and Saturday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. It provides access to printed books, academic journals, thesis, and digital resources to support student learning and research.

Events

NWSSU holds various events throughout the academic year including the University Foundation Day, Research Colloquium, Inter-College Sports Festival, and the Graduation Ceremony. Announcements are posted on the university bulletin boards and official social media pages.

Latest News

Stay updated with the latest announcements and achievements of Northwest Samar State University. Recent highlights include topping the regional board exam results, opening a new Science Laboratory Building, and signing partnerships with technology companies for student internships and scholarships.
